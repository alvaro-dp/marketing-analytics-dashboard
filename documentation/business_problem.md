# Business Problem

Many companies invest heavily in digital marketing campaigns but lack visibility into:

- which campaigns are profitable
- where budget waste occurs
- which segments are scalable
- where performance anomalies exist

This creates inefficient investment allocation and limits strategic decision-making.

The challenge is not the lack of data, but the inability to transform marketing metrics into actionable business insights.

---

# Business Objectives

The objective of this project is to build an executive-level Marketing Analytics system capable of:

- monitoring campaign profitability
- identifying underperforming campaigns
- detecting performance anomalies
- improving budget allocation
- supporting strategic decisions through KPIs

# KPI Definitions

This project focuses on profitability-oriented KPIs.

---

# ROAS

Return on Ad Spend.

Measures advertising profitability.

Formula:

ROAS = Revenue / Spend

---

# CPC

Cost per Click.

Measures traffic acquisition efficiency.

Formula:

CPC = Spend / Clicks

---

# CPA

Cost per Acquisition.

Measures conversion acquisition cost.

Formula:

CPA = Spend / Conversions

---

# CTR

Click Through Rate.

Measures ad attractiveness and engagement.

Formula:

CTR = Clicks / Impressions

---

# Profit

Measures net profitability.

Formula:

Profit = Revenue - Spend

---

# Conversion Rate

Measures conversion efficiency.

Formula:

Conversion Rate = Conversions / Clicks
